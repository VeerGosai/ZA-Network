Network Details

Unlike the other exchanges, NMBINX operates from a single location with a streamlined infrastructure:

  • Single facility deployment for efficient operations
  • Direct connections to participating networks
  • Serves the Nelson Mandela Bay metropolitan area

This focused approach allows for optimized connectivity in the Eastern Cape region.
