Network Details

This visualization shows the connections between switches at DINX's two locations in Durban:

  • RVH: Main River Horse Valley location with three interconnected switches (psw1, psw2, psw3)
  • UMH: Secondary uMhlanga location with one switch connected to two of the RVH switches

The topology demonstrates DINX's redundant network design with multiple connections between switches and locations.
